# Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana - Per Drop More Crop (PMKSY-PDMC)

**Ministry:** Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
**Launch Year:** 2015
**Type:** Micro Irrigation / Water Use Efficiency
**Official Website:** https://pmksy.gov.in/microirrigation/index.aspx

---

## Objective

Enhance water use efficiency in agriculture by promoting micro-irrigation technologies (drip and sprinkler irrigation), ensuring "Per Drop More Crop" to improve productivity while conserving water resources.

## Benefits

- Subsidy on drip and sprinkler irrigation systems
- Up to 55% subsidy for small and marginal farmers
- Up to 45% subsidy for other farmers
- Additional state top-up subsidies in many states
- Reduced water consumption by 30-50%
- Increased crop yield by 20-40%
- Reduced labor and fertilizer costs

## Eligible Activities

- Drip irrigation systems
- Sprinkler irrigation systems
- Micro-sprinklers and rain guns
- Micro-level water storage and conservation projects (new in 2025)
- Diggi construction and water harvesting systems
- Fertigation systems (fertilizer + irrigation)

## Eligibility

- All farmers with agricultural land
- Priority to small and marginal farmers
- SC/ST farmers get additional preference
- Farmers in water-scarce and drought-prone areas
- Groups of farmers, FPOs, and cooperatives

## How to Apply

1. Contact District Agriculture Office or Horticulture Department
2. Apply through the designated state portal or DBT Agriculture portal
3. Select irrigation system from approved suppliers
4. Get installation verified by officials
5. Subsidy credited to bank account after verification

## Documents Required

- Aadhaar Card
- Land ownership documents
- Bank account details
- Caste certificate (for SC/ST farmers)
- Water source availability proof

## FY 2025-26 Status

- Budget allocation: Rs 8,259.85 crore for PMKSY
- 96.83 lakh hectares covered under micro irrigation (2015-16 to 2025-26)
- Revised guidelines in 2025 expanding flexibility for states
- New provisions for micro-level water management activities
