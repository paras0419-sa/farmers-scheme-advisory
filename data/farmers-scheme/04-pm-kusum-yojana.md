# Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyaan (PM-KUSUM)

**Ministry:** Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE)
**Launch Year:** 2019
**Type:** Solar Energy for Agriculture
**Official Website:** https://pmkusum.mnre.gov.in/

---

## Objective

Promote the use of solar energy in the agriculture sector by providing reliable daytime solar power for irrigation, reducing dependence on grid supply and diesel, and generating additional income for farmers.

## Components

### Component A: Decentralized Solar Power Plants
- Installation of 10,000 MW of grid-connected solar/renewable power plants
- Capacity: 500 kW to 2 MW per plant
- Eligible: Individual farmers, cooperatives, panchayats, FPOs, solar power developers

### Component B: Standalone Off-Grid Solar Water Pumps
- Installation of 14 lakh standalone solar water pumps
- Capacity: up to 7.5 HP
- For farmers in remote/off-grid areas

### Component C: Solarization of Existing Grid-Connected Pumps
- Solarization of 35 lakh existing grid-connected agriculture pumps
- Two sub-components: individual pump solarization and feeder-level solarization

## Financial Support / Subsidy

**Regular States:**
- 30% Central Financial Assistance (CFA) from MNRE
- 30% State subsidy
- 40% farmer's share (can be availed as bank loan)

**Special Category States (NE states, Sikkim, J&K, Ladakh, HP, Uttarakhand, Lakshadweep, A&N):**
- 50% CFA from MNRE
- 30% State subsidy
- 20% farmer's share

## Eligibility

- Individual farmers owning agricultural land (including barren or semi-cultivable land)
- Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs)
- Cooperatives
- Panchayats
- Groups of farmers pooling land or resources
- Water User Associations

## How to Apply

1. Visit the PM-KUSUM national portal (pmkusum.mnre.gov.in)
2. Or apply through respective State Nodal Agency
3. Select the relevant component (A, B, or C)
4. Submit application with land and identity documents

## Documents Required

- Aadhaar Card
- Land ownership documents
- Bank account details
- Existing electricity connection details (for Component C)
- NOC from DISCOM (for Component A)

## FY 2025-26 Status

- Budget allocation: Rs 2,600 crore for 2025-26
- Component B: 4.4 lakh pumps installed in FY25 (4.2x increase over previous year)
- Component C: 2.6 lakh pumps solarized (25x increase over FY24)
- Current phase concludes March 2026; Phase II planned
