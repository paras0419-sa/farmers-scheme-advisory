# PM Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

**Ministry:** Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
**Launch Year:** 2019
**Type:** Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
**Official Website:** https://pmkisan.gov.in/

---

## Objective

Provide income support to all landholding farmer families across the country to supplement their financial needs for procuring various inputs related to agriculture and allied activities, as well as domestic needs.

## Benefits

- Rs 6,000 per year per farmer family
- Paid in 3 equal installments of Rs 2,000 each
- Transferred directly into Aadhaar-seeded bank accounts via DBT

## Eligibility Criteria

- Must own cultivable land as per land records of respective State/UT
- Valid Aadhaar number (Aadhaar authentication/eKYC is mandatory)
- Valid bank account number with IFSC code

## Exclusion Categories (NOT Eligible)

- Institutional landholders
- Families with members holding or having held constitutional posts (Ministers, MPs, MLAs, Mayors, Chairpersons of District Panchayats)
- Serving or retired officers and employees of Central/State Government Ministries
- Pensioners receiving monthly pension of Rs 10,000 or more (excluding Multi Tasking Staff / Class IV / Group D employees)
- Individuals who paid income tax in the previous assessment year
- Professionals such as doctors, engineers, lawyers, chartered accountants, and architects registered with professional bodies

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official PM-KISAN Portal at pmkisan.gov.in
2. Go to Farmers' Corner > New Farmer Registration
3. Enter Aadhaar number, fill in details, submit application
4. Can also register through Common Service Centres (CSCs) or via State Nodal Officers

## Documents Required

- Aadhaar Card
- Land ownership documents (Khasra/Khatauni)
- Bank account details with IFSC code
- Mobile number

## FY 2025-26 Status

- 22nd installment released on 13 March 2026
- Over Rs 18,640 crore disbursed to 9.32 crore farmer families
- Total beneficiaries since inception: over 11 crore farmer families
