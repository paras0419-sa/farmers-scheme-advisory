# Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM)

**Ministry:** Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
**Launch Year:** 2014-15
**Type:** Farm Mechanization Subsidy
**Official Website:** https://agrimachinery.nic.in/

---

## Objective

Promote adoption of appropriate, cost-effective, and environmentally friendly farm mechanization technologies, making modern machinery accessible and affordable, especially for small and marginal farmers.

## Benefits

- Subsidy of up to 50% for SC/ST, small, marginal, and women farmers
- Subsidy of up to 40% for other categories of farmers
- Access to farm machinery through Custom Hiring Centers (CHCs)
- Training on operation and maintenance of farm equipment
- Demonstration of new technologies including drones

## Key Components

### 1. Custom Hiring Centers (CHCs)
- Establishment of centers where farmers can rent farm machinery
- Reduces individual cost of owning expensive equipment

### 2. Village Level Farm Machinery Banks (VLFMBs)
- Community-owned machinery for shared use
- Promotes cooperative farming

### 3. High-Tech Hubs (HTHs)
- Advanced agricultural technology demonstration and training centers

### 4. Procurement Subsidy
- Direct financial assistance for purchasing farm machinery and equipment

### 5. Drone Technology (New in 2025)
- Purchase subsidies for agricultural drones
- Custom Hiring Centers for drones
- Drone-based demonstrations and remote spraying

## Eligible Equipment

- Tractors and power tillers
- Rotavators, cultivators, and seed drills
- Combine harvesters and threshers
- Sprayers and plant protection equipment
- Agricultural drones
- Post-harvest processing equipment
- Precision agriculture instruments

## Eligibility

- Individual farmers
- Small and marginal farmers (priority)
- SC/ST farmers and women farmers (higher subsidy)
- FPOs, cooperatives, and SHGs (for CHCs and VLFMBs)
- State government agencies and institutions (for HTHs)
- One machine per farmer every 3 years

## How to Apply

1. Visit the SMAM portal at agrimachinery.nic.in
2. Register using Aadhaar number
3. Select desired machinery and apply
4. Application verified by district officials
5. Purchase from empaneled dealers
6. Subsidy credited after verification

## Documents Required

- Aadhaar Card
- Land records
- Bank account details
- Caste certificate (for SC/ST farmers)
- Passport-size photographs

## FY 2025-26 Status

- Revised operational guidelines issued May 2025
- Drone technologies explicitly included under SMAM
- Focus on precision agriculture and modern tools
