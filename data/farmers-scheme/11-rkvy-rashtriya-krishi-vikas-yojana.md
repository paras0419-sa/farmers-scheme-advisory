# Pradhan Mantri Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (PM-RKVY)

**Ministry:** Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
**Launch Year:** 2007 (Renamed PM-RKVY in 2025)
**Type:** State-level Agricultural Development
**Official Website:** http://rkvy.nic.in/

---

## Objective

Incentivize states to increase public investment in agriculture and allied sectors, provide flexibility to plan and execute agriculture programs based on local needs, reduce yield gaps, and support agri-business entrepreneurship.

## Benefits

- Flexible funding to states for agriculture development
- Support for agri-infrastructure creation
- Promotion of agri-entrepreneurship and startups
- Innovation funding for new farming technologies
- Strengthening of agricultural value chains

## Key Components

### 1. Regular RKVY (Flexi Fund)
- 20% of annual outlay as flexible funds for states
- States can plan based on local agricultural priorities

### 2. National Priority Sub-Schemes
- 20% of annual outlay for special schemes of national importance

### 3. Innovation and Agri-Entrepreneur Development
- 10% of annual outlay (including 2% administrative costs)
- Support for agri-startups and innovations
- Incubation support and mentoring

## Eligible Activities

- Farm mechanization and technology adoption
- Soil health improvement and organic farming
- Crop diversification and value addition
- Livestock, poultry, and fisheries development
- Post-harvest management infrastructure
- Agri-marketing and export promotion
- Agri-startup funding and incubation

## Fund Sharing Pattern

| Region | Centre : State |
|--------|---------------|
| Regular states | 60:40 |
| North Eastern & Himalayan states | 90:10 |

## Eligibility

- State governments (primary implementing agency)
- Farmers benefit through state-level agricultural programs
- Agri-startups and entrepreneurs (through innovation component)

## FY 2025-26 Status

- Budget allocation: Rs 8,500 crore (increased from Rs 7,150 crore in 2023-24)
- Renamed to PM-RKVY with focus on sustainable farming and innovation
- Revised operational guidelines issued January 28, 2025
