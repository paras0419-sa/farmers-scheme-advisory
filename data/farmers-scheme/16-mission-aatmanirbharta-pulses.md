# Mission for Aatmanirbharta in Pulses

**Ministry:** Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
**Launch Year:** 2025
**Type:** Pulse Production Self-Sufficiency
**Official Website:** https://www.pib.gov.in/

---

## Objective

Achieve self-sufficiency in pulse production in India with special focus on Tur (Arhar/Pigeon Pea), Urad (Black Gram), and Masoor (Lentil) through guaranteed procurement, assured prices, and production support.

## Benefits

- Guaranteed procurement by central agencies NAFED and NCCF for 4 years
- Assured prices for pulse farmers
- Production support and technical assistance
- Seed distribution and varietal improvement
- Reduced import dependence on pulses

## Key Features

1. **Guaranteed Procurement** — NAFED and NCCF will procure Tur, Urad, and Masoor from registered farmers for 4 years
2. **Price Assurance** — Farmers guaranteed MSP-linked prices, removing price crash risk
3. **Registration-Based** — Farmers must register with NAFED/NCCF to avail procurement benefit
4. **6-Year Mission** — Long-term commitment to pulse production enhancement

## Target Crops

| Crop | Common Name | Season |
|------|------------|--------|
| Tur / Arhar | Pigeon Pea | Kharif |
| Urad | Black Gram | Kharif / Rabi |
| Masoor | Lentil | Rabi |

## Eligibility

- All farmers growing Tur, Urad, or Masoor
- Must register with NAFED or NCCF
- Available across all major pulse-growing states

## How to Participate

1. Register with NAFED or NCCF (online or through local procurement centers)
2. Grow Tur, Urad, or Masoor as per recommended practices
3. Bring produce to designated procurement centers
4. Get guaranteed purchase at MSP or above

## Duration

- 6-year mission period
- Procurement guarantee for first 4 years
- Announced in Union Budget 2025-26
