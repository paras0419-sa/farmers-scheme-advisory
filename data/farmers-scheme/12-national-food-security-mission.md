# National Food Security Mission (NFSM)

**Ministry:** Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
**Launch Year:** 2007
**Type:** Crop Production Enhancement
**Official Website:** https://www.nfsm.gov.in/

---

## Objective

Increase production of rice, wheat, pulses, coarse cereals, nutri-cereals, and commercial crops (cotton, jute, sugarcane) through area expansion and productivity enhancement in a sustainable manner.

## Benefits

- Subsidized distribution of certified/high-yielding seeds
- Assistance for micronutrients and soil ameliorants
- Support for farm mechanization (equipment and tools)
- Cluster demonstrations for improved farming practices
- Training and capacity building programs
- Integrated Pest Management (IPM) demonstrations
- Support for water saving devices and plant protection measures

## Crops Covered

| Category | Crops |
|----------|-------|
| Cereals | Rice, Wheat, Coarse Cereals, Nutri-Cereals (Millets) |
| Pulses | Tur/Arhar, Moong, Urad, Chana, Masoor, Lentil |
| Oilseeds | Soybean, Groundnut, Rapeseed-Mustard, Sunflower |
| Commercial | Cotton, Jute, Sugarcane |

## Key Interventions

1. **Seed distribution** — High-yielding and climate-resilient varieties at subsidized rates
2. **Demonstrations** — Cluster demonstrations on farmers' fields to showcase best practices
3. **Mechanization** — Distribution of farm implements and machinery
4. **Nutrient management** — Micronutrients, bio-fertilizers, gypsum, lime application
5. **Plant protection** — IPM demonstrations and bio-agent distribution
6. **Cropping system-based training** — For farmers and extension workers

## Eligibility

- All farmers in NFSM-identified districts (638 districts covered)
- Priority to small and marginal farmers
- SC/ST farmers receive additional preference

## How to Apply

- Contact District Agriculture Office
- Apply through State Agriculture Department portal
- Assistance provided based on crop-wise and area-wise allocation

## Documents Required

- Aadhaar Card
- B1 Khasra (irrigation certificate)
- Land records
- Bank passbook
- Caste certificate (for SC/ST farmers)

## FY 2025-26 Status

- Coverage: 638 districts across India
- Budget allocation: Rs 9.65 crore for 2025-26
- Focus on increasing pulse and oilseed production for self-sufficiency
