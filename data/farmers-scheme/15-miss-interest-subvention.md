# Modified Interest Subvention Scheme (MISS)

**Ministry:** Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare / Department of Financial Services
**Launch Year:** Ongoing (continued for FY 2025-26)
**Type:** Agricultural Credit Subsidy
**Official Website:** https://www.pib.gov.in/PressReleasePage.aspx?PRID=2131989

---

## Objective

Ensure availability of short-term agricultural credit to farmers at an affordable interest rate through Kisan Credit Cards (KCC), reducing farmers' cost of borrowing and encouraging timely repayment.

## Benefits

- 1.5% interest subvention to banks on short-term agri loans through KCC
- Loans at concessional rate of 7% per annum (instead of market rates)
- Prompt Repayment Incentive (PRI) of 3% for timely repayers
- Effective interest rate reduced to 4% for prompt-paying farmers
- Coverage for crop cultivation, animal husbandry, and fisheries

## How It Works

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Normal bank interest rate | ~9-10% per annum |
| Government interest subvention | -1.5% |
| Rate for farmer | 7% per annum |
| Prompt Repayment Incentive | -3% |
| **Effective rate for prompt repayers** | **4% per annum** |

## Loan Limits

| Category | Limit |
|----------|-------|
| Short-term crop loans (existing) | Up to Rs 3 lakh |
| Short-term crop loans (Budget 2025-26 proposal) | Up to Rs 5 lakh |
| Animal husbandry / fisheries loans | Up to Rs 2 lakh |

## Eligibility

- All farmers availing short-term crop loans through KCC
- Fishermen with KCC
- Dairy farmers with KCC
- Must repay within the crop season/year to avail PRI benefit

## Implementing Institutions

- Public Sector Banks
- Private Sector Banks
- Regional Rural Banks (RRBs)
- Cooperative Banks
- Jointly implemented by RBI and NABARD

## FY 2025-26 Status

- Cabinet approved continuation for FY 2025-26
- Budget allocation: Rs 15,640 crore
- Benefits approximately 7.7 crore farmers
- Loan limit proposed to increase to Rs 5 lakh
