# National Mission on Edible Oils - Oil Palm (NMEO-OP)

**Ministry:** Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
**Launch Year:** 2021
**Type:** Edible Oil Self-Sufficiency
**Official Website:** https://nmeo.dac.gov.in/

---

## Objective

Enhance domestic edible oil production by expanding oil palm cultivation area, increasing crude palm oil (CPO) production, and reducing India's dependence on imported edible oils.

## Benefits

- Price assurance for Fresh Fruit Bunches (FFB) through Viability Price (VP) mechanism
- Protection from international CPO price fluctuations
- Increased planting material assistance: Rs 29,000 per hectare (up from Rs 12,000)
- Maintenance cost assistance for initial years
- Free distribution of quality planting material

## Targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Additional oil palm area | 6.5 lakh hectares by 2025-26 |
| Total oil palm area | 10 lakh hectares |
| CPO production by 2025-26 | 11.20 lakh tonnes |
| CPO production by 2029-30 | 28 lakh tonnes |

## Financial Allocation

- Total outlay: Rs 11,040 crore
- Central Government share: Rs 8,844 crore
- State Government share: Rs 2,196 crore

## Eligibility

- Farmers with land suitable for oil palm cultivation
- Special focus on North East region and Andaman & Nicobar Islands
- States with high potential: Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Gujarat, Mizoram, Nagaland, Arunachal Pradesh

## Key Features

1. **Viability Price (VP)** — Government-guaranteed floor price for FFB, protecting farmers from price crashes
2. **Enhanced input support** — Substantially increased assistance for planting material
3. **NE Region focus** — Special incentives for cultivation in North East India
4. **Industry partnership** — Collaboration with oil palm processing companies for buyback agreements

## How to Apply

1. Contact State Horticulture/Agriculture Department
2. Register through the NMEO portal (nmeo.dac.gov.in)
3. Connect with empaneled oil palm companies in your region

## Documents Required

- Aadhaar Card
- Land documents showing suitability for oil palm
- Bank account details

## FY 2025-26 Status

- Coverage increased from 3.70 lakh hectares (2020-21) to 5.56 lakh hectares (March 2025)
- 1.89 lakh hectares achieved under the Mission alone
