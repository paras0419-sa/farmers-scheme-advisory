# Pradhan Mantri Dhan-Dhaanya Krishi Yojana (PMDDKY)

**Ministry:** Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare (with 11 ministries)
**Launch Year:** 2025
**Type:** Integrated Agricultural Development
**Official Website:** https://pmddky.com/

---

## Objective

Revolutionize Indian agriculture by enhancing productivity, improving irrigation, facilitating credit access, and promoting sustainable farming in India's 100 most underperforming agricultural districts.

## Benefits

- Comprehensive support covering irrigation, storage, loans, training, market access, and modern technology
- Convergence of 36 existing agricultural schemes across 11 ministries
- Digital dashboard tracking 117 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Targeted support for small and marginal farmers (< 2 hectares)

## Key Features

1. **Irrigation improvement** — Expand irrigation coverage in target districts
2. **Credit facilitation** — Both long-term and short-term agricultural credit
3. **Storage infrastructure** — Post-harvest storage to reduce crop losses
4. **Training and capacity building** — Modern farming techniques
5. **Market access** — Better connectivity to markets and buyers
6. **Technology adoption** — Precision agriculture and digital tools

## Target

- 100 districts with low productivity, moderate crop intensity, and below-average credit parameters
- Approximately 1.7 crore farmers to benefit
- Focus on small and marginal farmers, women, and youth

## Budget

- Annual budget: Rs 24,000 crore
- Duration: 6 years (2025-26 to 2030-31)
- Total outlay: Rs 1.44 lakh crore

## Eligibility

- Farmers in the 100 identified target districts
- Priority to small and marginal farmers (< 2 hectares)
- Women farmers
- Youth in agriculture

## Implementation Timeline

- Announced: February 1, 2025 (Union Budget 2025-26)
- Cabinet approval: July 16, 2025
- Launch: October 2025 (Rabi season)
- Applications: September 2025 onwards

## Monitoring

- Digital dashboard with 117 KPIs including crop yields, loan disbursals, and storage usage
- District-level implementation committees
- Tailored approach based on local needs
