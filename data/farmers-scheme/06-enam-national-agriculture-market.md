# e-National Agriculture Market (eNAM)

**Ministry:** Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
**Launch Year:** 2016
**Type:** Online Trading Platform
**Official Website:** https://enam.gov.in/

---

## Objective

Create a unified national market for agricultural commodities by networking existing APMC mandis across the country through an electronic trading portal, ensuring better prices for farmers through transparent price discovery and reduced intermediaries.

## Benefits

- Transparent online bidding and price discovery
- Access to more buyers and better prices
- Direct electronic payment into farmer bank accounts
- Reduced dependence on middlemen and commission agents
- Real-time price information across mandis
- Quality assaying facilities at mandis
- Single trading license valid across states

## How It Works

1. Farmer brings produce to an eNAM-enabled mandi
2. Produce is weighed and quality-tested (assayed)
3. Details uploaded on the eNAM platform
4. Traders across multiple mandis can bid online
5. Highest bidder wins
6. Payment transferred directly to farmer's bank account

## Eligibility

- All farmers with agricultural produce
- Traders registered with APMCs
- FPOs (Farmer Producer Organizations)
- Bulk buyers, processors, and exporters
- Commission agents (Arthiyas) registered on the platform

## Coverage (as of 2025-26)

- 1,389 mandis integrated across 23 States and 4 Union Territories
- 100+ commodities traded (grains, pulses, oilseeds, fruits, vegetables, spices)
- 1.77 crore+ farmers registered
- 2.53 lakh+ traders registered

## Commodities Covered

Grains (wheat, rice, maize), Pulses (tur, chana, urad, moong), Oilseeds (soybean, groundnut, mustard), Spices (turmeric, chilli, coriander), Fruits and Vegetables, Cotton, Jute, and more.

## How to Register

1. Visit enam.gov.in
2. Register as Farmer / Trader / FPO
3. Provide Aadhaar, bank details, and mandi registration
4. Can also register through nearest eNAM-enabled mandi

## Documents Required

- Aadhaar Card
- Bank account details
- Mobile number
- Mandi registration details (for traders)

## 2025-26 Enhancements

- Logistics integration with Reefer vehicles and WDRA warehouses
- FPOs eligible for Rs 5,000/MT transport subsidy under PM-KISAN linkages
- Improved mobile app for real-time price updates
