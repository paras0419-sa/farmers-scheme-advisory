# Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme

**Ministry:** Ministry of Finance / Department of Financial Services
**Launch Year:** 1998 (Modified in 2012 and 2025)
**Type:** Agricultural Credit
**Official Website:** https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/kcc

---

## Objective

Provide adequate and timely credit support from the banking system to farmers for their cultivation and other needs including purchase of agricultural inputs, draw cash for production needs, and repayment of loans.

## Benefits

- Short-term crop loans at subsidized interest rate of 7% per annum
- Prompt repayment incentive (PRI) of 3%, reducing effective rate to 4%
- Collateral-free loans up to Rs 2 lakh
- Coverage includes crop cultivation, post-harvest expenses, produce marketing, consumption needs, working capital for farm assets, and investment credit for agriculture and allied activities
- Insurance coverage under PMFBY for crop loans
- Personal accident insurance cover of Rs 50,000 (death) and Rs 25,000 (disability)

## 2025-26 Key Updates

- Loan limit under Modified Interest Subvention Scheme increased from Rs 3 lakh to Rs 5 lakh
- Covers fishermen, dairy farmers, and poultry farmers in addition to crop farmers
- Benefit reaches approximately 7.7 crore farmers

## Eligibility Criteria

- Individual farmers (owner cultivators)
- Small farmers, marginal farmers
- Share croppers, oral lessees, and tenant farmers
- Self Help Groups (SHGs) or Joint Liability Groups (JLGs) of farmers
- Minimum age: 18 years
- Maximum age: 75 years (co-borrower required if above 60, must be legal heir)

## How to Apply

1. Visit nearest branch of any Commercial Bank, Regional Rural Bank, Small Finance Bank, or Cooperative Bank
2. Fill out the KCC application form
3. Submit required documents
4. Bank processes and issues the KCC within 14 days

## Documents Required

- Aadhaar Card
- PAN Card
- Voter ID / Driving License (identity proof)
- Land records / ownership documents
- Passport-size photographs
- Bank account details

## Interest Structure

| Component | Rate |
|-----------|------|
| Base interest rate | 7% per annum |
| Government interest subvention | -1.5% |
| Prompt Repayment Incentive | -3% |
| **Effective rate for prompt repayers** | **4% per annum** |

## Implementing Banks

- Commercial Banks (SBI, PNB, etc.)
- Regional Rural Banks (RRBs)
- Small Finance Banks
- Cooperative Banks

## FY 2025-26 Status

- Operative KCC amount crosses Rs 10 lakh crore
- Benefits 7.72 crore farmers nationwide
- Rs 15,640 crore allocated for Modified Interest Subvention Scheme
