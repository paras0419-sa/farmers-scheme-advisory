# Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

**Ministry:** Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
**Launch Year:** 2016
**Type:** Crop Insurance
**Official Website:** https://pmfby.gov.in/

---

## Objective

Provide comprehensive insurance coverage against crop failure due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases, stabilizing farmer income and encouraging adoption of innovative farming practices.

## Benefits

- Comprehensive yield-based crop insurance cover
- Coverage against drought, dry spells, floods, inundation, hailstorm, landslides, natural fire, lightning, pest attacks, and diseases
- Low premium rates for farmers; government subsidizes the rest

## Premium Rates (Farmer's Share)

| Season | Crop Type | Farmer Premium |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| Kharif | Food & oilseed crops | 2% of sum insured |
| Rabi | Food & oilseed crops | 1.5% of sum insured |
| Both | Commercial & horticulture crops | 5% of sum insured |

The remaining actuarial premium is shared equally between the Central and State Governments.

## Eligibility Criteria

- All farmers growing notified crops in notified areas
- Both loanee and non-loanee farmers can enroll
- Sharecroppers and tenant farmers are also eligible (with documents)
- Enrollment is voluntary for non-loanee farmers

## How to Apply

1. Through nearest bank branch, CSC, or insurance company office
2. Online via the PMFBY portal (pmfby.gov.in)
3. Via mobile app — "Crop Insurance" app on Android/iOS
4. Through designated insurance company representatives

## Documents Required

- Aadhaar Card
- Bank passbook
- Land records (Khasra number, land area, crop sown details)
- Sowing certificate from Patwari/Revenue official
- Declaration of crop sown (for non-loanee farmers)

## Enrollment Windows

- **Kharif season:** April to July (varies by state)
- **Rabi season:** October to December (varies by state)
- Rabi 2025-26: Registration open 1 December to 31 December 2025

## Claim Process

1. Report crop loss within 72 hours to insurance company, bank, or via the Crop Insurance App
2. Claim assessment done through Crop Cutting Experiments (CCE) or remote sensing technology
3. Claims settled into farmer's bank account directly

## FY 2025-26 Status

- Scheme extended till 2025-26 with outlay of Rs 69,515.71 crore
- New Krishi Rakshak Portal and Helpline (KRPH) launched for grievance redressal
- Over 50 million farmer applications annually
