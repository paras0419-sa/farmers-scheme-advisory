# Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)

**Ministry:** Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
**Launch Year:** 2020
**Type:** Credit / Infrastructure Financing
**Official Website:** https://agriinfra.dac.gov.in/

---

## Objective

Mobilize medium-long term debt financing for investment in viable post-harvest management infrastructure and community farming assets through interest subvention and credit guarantee support.

## Benefits

- 3% per annum interest subvention on loans up to Rs 2 crore
- Credit guarantee coverage under CGTMSE for loans up to Rs 2 crore
- Loan repayment period of 7 years (including moratorium of 6 months to 2 years)
- Minimum owner's contribution: only 10% of fixed project cost

## Eligible Infrastructure Projects

- Warehouses and cold storage facilities
- Grading and sorting units
- Primary processing centers (cleaning, grading, packing)
- Ripening chambers
- Supply chain infrastructure (e-marketing platforms, farm gate infrastructure)
- Custom Hiring Centers for farm equipment
- Organic input production units
- Smart and precision agriculture projects
- Solar projects under PM-KUSUM Component-A (for FPOs, cooperatives, panchayats)

## Eligibility

- Individual farmers
- Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs)
- PACS (Primary Agricultural Credit Societies)
- Marketing Cooperative Societies
- Self Help Groups (SHGs) and Federations of SHGs
- Joint Liability Groups (JLGs)
- Agri-entrepreneurs and startups
- Central/State agency or Local Body sponsored PPP projects
- APMCs (Agricultural Produce Market Committees)
- State Agencies and Federations of Cooperatives

## How to Apply

1. Visit the AIF portal at agriinfra.dac.gov.in
2. Register and create an account
3. Submit project proposal with required documents
4. Application routed to bank for loan approval
5. Interest subvention applied automatically on disbursement

## Documents Required

- Aadhaar Card and PAN Card
- Detailed Project Report (DPR)
- Land documents (ownership/lease)
- Bank account details
- Entity registration documents (for FPOs, cooperatives, etc.)

## Fund Allocation

- Total scheme size: Rs 1 lakh crore (till 2025-26)
- As of June 2025: Rs 66,310 crore sanctioned for 1,13,419 projects
- Total investment mobilized: Rs 1,07,502 crore
